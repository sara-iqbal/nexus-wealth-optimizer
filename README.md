## **Nexus Wealth: Strategic Capital & Balance Sheet Optimizer**


Nexus Wealth is an interactive Decision Support System (DSS) designed for Global Balance Sheet Management within the Wealth Management sector. This tool automates the assessment of capital utilization, allowing stakeholders to optimize Risk-Weighted Assets (RWA) and maximize Return on Risk-Weighted Assets (RoRWA) across diverse client segments.

This project was built to demonstrate proficiency in financial modeling, regulatory capital standards (Basel III), and dynamic data visualization—core competencies required for high-level analytical roles at institutions like Citi.

# **Key Features**

**1.Integrated Return Tool (Pricing Engine)**

Dynamic Pricing: Real-time assessment of new deal profitability against a 12% Capital Hurdle.


Regulatory Logic: Automates RWA calculations using Basel III standardized weights for various products (Lombard Loans, Mortgages, Structured Lending).


Segment Awareness: Tailored for Ultra High Net Worth (UHNW), Family Offices, and Professional Firms.

**2.Capital Efficiency Frontier**

Visual Strategy: A 4-quadrant scatter analysis mapping product risk against expected returns.


Optimization: Identifies "Capital Efficiency Winners" products with low risk-weights but high margins (e.g., Lombard Loans).

**3.Economic Profit Waterfall**

Transparency: Visualizes the erosion of gross revenue through Capital Charges and Operating Expenses to arrive at true Net Economic Profit.

# **Technical Implementation**
